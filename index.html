Fury Unleashed

Welcome to Fury Unleashed—a world where chaos reigns and every duel is a test of will, skill, and survival. In this brutal arena, warriors from across the realms collide, each unleashing their fury in a fight for dominance. Choose your fighter, harness their unique powers, and rise through the ranks in a clash where only the fiercest can prevail.

Info

Player One Controls:

Use A to move left, D to move right, W to jump, C for a light attack, and V for a heavy attack.

Player Two Controls:

Make sure Num Lock is on. Use 4 to move left, 6 to move right, 8 to jump, "/" for a light attack, and "." for a heavy attack — both located on the QWERTY side of the keyboard.

Instructions:

  • Choose a battle format.
  • Set timer between 1 and 600 seconds.
  • Set HP no less than 50.
  • Set damage no less than 1.
  • You can select your character from the available options.
  • Set crit chance between 0 and 0.5.
  • Set crit damage between 1.5 and 2.5

Note:

  • Heavy attacks deal double the damage of light attacks, have a chance to crit, but come with a 5-second cooldown.
  • Critical hits inflict 1.5 times more damage than normal by default.
